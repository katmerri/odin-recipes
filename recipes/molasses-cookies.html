Molasses Cookies

Super Soft Molasses Cookies

This molasses cookies recipe from Sally's Baking Addiction is delicious and easy to follow. These cookies are seriously soft and chewy and easy to bake.

Ingredients:
  • 2 1/4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 2 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 1 1/4 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Cloves
  • 1/4 tsp Nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 Cup Unsalted Butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 Cup packed Light Brown Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Dark Molasses
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
Rolling:
  • 1/3 Cup Granulated Sugar, for rolling
Instructions:
  1. Whisk the flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, nutmeg, and salt together until combined well. Set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl beat the butter and both sugars on high speed until creamy and combined, about 2 minutes. Add the molasses and beat until combined. Then add the egg and vanilla extract and beat until combined, about 1 minute. Scrape down sides and bottom of bowl as needed.
  3. On low speed, slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until combined. The cookie dough will be slightly sticky. Cover dough tightly with aluminum foil or plastic wrap and chill for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line two large baking sheets with aluminum foil. Set aside.
  5. Remove cookie dough from the fridge. If the cookie dough chilled longer than 2 hours, let it sit at room temperatur for at least 30 minutes. The cookies may not spread in the oven if the dough is that cold. Roll dough, 1 Tbsp each, into balls. Roll each ball in granulated sugar and arrange 3 inches apart on the baking sheets.
  6. Bake for 11-12 minutes or until edges appear set. If the tops aren't appearing cracked as pictured, remove the baking sheet from the oven and gently bang on the counter 2-3x. This will help those warm cookies spread out and crack on top. Return to over.
  7. Remove cookies from oven and allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Enjoy, cookies will stay fresh covered for 1 week.
