Homemade Applesauce

Made with fresh apples picked from a small orchard in Vermont, this applesauce is sweet and delicious when served warm fresh from the stove top or chilled from the fridge.

Ingredients:
  • 4 Cortland Apples, chopped
  • 3/4 Cup Water
  • 1/4 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
Instructions:
  1. In a medium sized sauce pan add ingredients and mix together.
  2. Heat mixture to a boil and lower to low to medium heat and let cook for 20 minutes.
  3. Stir mixture, there may be a few chunks of apple left. If that's how you enjoy it, then enjoy!
  4. To get rid of chunks: transfer sauce to strainer over a bowl. Push sauce through strainer with a pestle or other like device.
  5. Once finished, enjoy! Or save for later.
